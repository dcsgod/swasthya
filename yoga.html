Bhujangasana

Bhujangasana is a back bending pose which is also a part of the traditional Surya Namaskar sequence coming at step 8, and step 20.

Chakrasana

Chakrasana, also known by the names of Wheel Pose, Half Moon Pose, Urdhva Dhanurasana and Upward-Facing Bow Pose is a backbend asana in yoga.

Ardha Matsyendrasana

Ardha Matsyendrasana, also known as the Spinal Twist increases the elasticity of the spine due to the twist happens while doing the posture.

Parvatasana

Parvatasana is part of the Surya Namasakar series of asanas. Parvatasana can strengthen the wrists and legs and increases the flow of blood to the head.

Natarajasana

Natarajasana or Lord of the Dance Pose is a standing back-bending asana. This pose is derived from a pose in the classical Indian dance form Bharatnatyam.

Akarna Dhanurasana

Akarna Dhanurasana is a regular practice of this asana helps to improve the flexibility of leg muscles and hips. It also improves the health of abdominal muscles.
